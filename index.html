Project Idea

Overview:

This project is a system which have two part, one for company system, another one for national system. This system can accurately locate the users of electronic products (mobile phones, computers, tablets), and determine the user's identity and appearance, even VPN cannot escape locating. This project is mainly used to punish and control online fraudsters. The responsibility of the national system is control and search, and this system is mainly installed in the police station. The responsibility of the company system is to collect data (identity, appearance, fingerprints, voice), and company system is mainly installed in a security company with national backgrounds.

Motivation:

This idea from two point. First, Internet fraud and mobile phone fraud, so many years, there are lot information from internet that someone was scammed, The most common example is that there are so many emails about fraud and preventive measures in the school mailbox and daily fraud phone call (Courier company and bank card lost). They need to be searched. Second, Imprisonment is inhumane for these prisoners after committing a crime, and no one can promise they will not do it again, because gain is bigger than pain. So, it is necessary for these people to be limited by using this system to check and control effectively.

Description:

This system main have two part which is used by company and country. For the police company, the main responsibility is to collect data, mainly through cooperation with various social software companies (WeChat, Facebook, Instagram) and Electronics Company (HON HAI PRECISION INDUSTRY, SAMSUNG and SONY ), this system is implanted in the background to automatically collect the identity information of every people, but this part of the function should be a strict confidential system, and explain the degree of protection to the outside world. For example, the controller has no right to read the information unless someone have a possibility of committing crimes, this system will open to police. Generally speaking, they are 100% protected if they do nothing wrong. Another function is open to public, everyone have the right to mark phone number or website or the information of someone who may be the scammer into this system as data, if something be marked more than a rate, this will be targeted by the system, using AI algorithms to predict whether it is possible to commit an online crime, and then the police will use this system to do some search. Also, people can report directly online by showing relevant evidence of being deceived. For national system, the mainly responsibility is to supervision and arrest. In the part of supervision, criminal’s device will shut down automatedly. System can detect every device’s sensor, compare and match the data of prisoners in the database every second, if people, who is limited on using device, keep using another device, looking at device more than 5 second, that device will shut down automatedly(General punishment), if criminal walk outside and be checked by others device, there will be a “!”in the left top of line in your device and notice everyone around your device (sharing information). Also, this system can be used to protect teenagers away form addition, like every teenager only can play games 2hours every day (time depend on different age), if more than 2 hours, the publish is similar to the criminal’s punishment. In the part of arrest, this system will open more right to check and search, by using different kinds of AI technology and the data from monitor, fingerprint database, chat history, purchase history, even online search history, every internet traces, calculate his method, motivation, and the place now criminal is. The most important and creative part is to find the VPN user’s location and keep locate in the online map database, but it is also the most difficult part, the other part can be found in many different apps and device system.

Tools and Technologies:

First, this system needs to have large number of databases (should store every internet history in one year and delated part) and server to store personal information like voice, appearance and fingerprint (nobody has right to search and check) which is used to match the people around the device. Second, all the sensor (Screen, touchpad and voice recorder) in device should keep working without any reaction, and it is necessary to have a micro camera in the screen, because criminal may cover the outside camera. Third, this system should have all the right to device like police search warrant to keep sensor open. Last, this system should work on every smart device, so that need to negotiate with every electronic product companies and software companies.
